# Results

Curated small outputs — result tables (CSV), summary statistics (JSON), figures (PNG), and protein structures — organized by phase. Large intermediate objects (cell-level `.h5ad`/`.h5mu` subsets, per-cell JSON dumps, checkpoints, logs) are **not** included; they are regenerated by the scripts in `../code/`.

## By phase

### `discover/` — Phase A exploratory audit
Dataset-level overview figures and summary tables (`discover_summary_layers.csv`, `discover_summary_metrics.csv`).

### `phaseA/` — Foundation
- `high_confidence_targets.csv` — the reproducible, on-target, off-target-clean nomination universe (12,576 perturbations / 5,728 genes).
- `de_schema.csv`, `qc_summary.json`, `reproducibility_summary.json`, `positive_controls.csv` — QC/schema, reproducibility, and positive-control benchmark.
- Figures: `qc_overview.png`, `reproducibility.png`, `positive_controls.png`.

### `phaseBC/` — Discovery + integration
- **`TARGET_SCORECARD.csv`** — the primary deliverable: 301 convergent candidates × integrated evidence columns.
- Per-direction tables: `cytokine_regulators*.csv`, `polarization_regulators.csv`, `context_specific_targets.csv`, `regulator_network_hubs.csv`, `gwas_regulator_overlap.csv`, `druggability_annotation.csv`, and the candidate-union tables.
- Figures: `top_targets_overview.png`, `cytokine_regulators_bars.png`, `polarization_heatmap.png`, `context_heatmap.png`, `regulator_network.png`, `network_modules_heatmap.png`, `gwas_disease_regulator.png`, `druggability_overview.png`.

### `phaseD/` — Single-cell validation (donor D4)
Knockdown efficiency, single-cell vs pseudobulk concordance, stimulation-dependence, state-shift, and validation tables (`sc_*.csv`, `flagship_pseudobulk_signatures.json`) + figures (`kd_efficiency.png`, `sc_vs_pseudobulk_concordance.png`, `stim_dependence.png`, `state_manifold.png`, `target_hit_pattern.png`).

### `phaseE/` — Cross-donor + novel-axis validation
- **E1** (flagship cross-donor): `E1_cross_donor_verdict.csv`, `E1_flagship_cross_donor_tidy.csv`, `E1_cross_donor.png`.
- **E2** (novel-axis validation): `E2_novel_axis_verdict.csv`, `E2_stim_dependence.csv`, `E2_novel_axis.png`.
- **E3** (state-manifold contrast): `E3_*.csv/json`, `E3_state_manifold.png`, plus the rigorous per-donor rebuild (`phaseE_E3_crossdonor_rigorous.*`, `phaseE_perdonor_E3_contrast.csv`, `phaseE_E3_coverage_control.csv`).

### `phaseF/` — Translational dossiers (19 targets)
- **`phaseF_master_druggability.csv`** — integrated structure + chemistry + tractability + clinical + genetics table.
- `phaseF_readiness_ranked.json`, `phaseF_trackA_signaling.json`, `phaseF_trackB_chromatin_tf.json`, `phaseF_plddt_profiles.json`.
- Figures: `phaseF_readiness_ranking.png`, `phaseF_druggability_landscape.png`, `phaseF_trackA_structure_maps.png`, `phaseF_trackB_structure_maps.png`.
- **`structures/`** — 19 AlphaFold models (`AF_<GENE>_<UniProt>.pdb`) + 6 representative experimental structures (`PDB_<GENE>_<PDBID>.{pdb,cif}`). Open in any molecular viewer (e.g. Mol*, PyMOL).

### `phaseG/` — Deep single-cell analysis (3 tiers, 19 targets, 4 donors)
Three readouts per context (Rest / Stim8hr / Stim48hr) — `G_dose_response_*.csv`, `G_responder_*.csv`, `G_baseline_sensitivity_*.csv` — plus cross-donor differential abundance (`G4_*`, `G5_*`), rare-state concordance (`G6_*`), co-expression rewiring (`G8_*`), activation-trajectory checkpoint (`G9_*`), and dual-guide concordance (`G10_*`), each with its figure.

## Figure numbering

Figures are referenced by their consolidated-report numbers (Fig A1, B1–B6, C1, D1–D4, E1–E4, F1–F4, G0/G4–G10) in `../docs/FULL_REPORT.md`. Filenames here are the descriptive source names.
